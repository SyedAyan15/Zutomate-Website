# Skill: Copy & Tone for Zutomate

## Brand Voice
- **Direct.** No fluff, no filler sentences.
- **Confident.** Not arrogant. We know what we do well.
- **B2B professional.** Speaks to founders, heads of sales, ops leads.
- **Grounded.** No hype words. Results-focused.

## What Zutomate Does (always use these framings)
- Builds **AI Growth Systems** — not just automation
- Full-stack GTM: outbound, CRM, AI agents, chatbots
- Works across Clay, Claygent, Apollo, Smartlead, HeyReach, n8n, Claude API
- Serves B2B businesses that want predictable pipeline

## Hero Tagline
> "We Build Predictable Growth Systems for B2B Businesses"

Sub: "Embedding intelligent AI workflows into businesses to engineer end-to-end growth systems."

## Banned Words / Phrases
- "cutting-edge" / "state-of-the-art"
- "innovative" / "innovation"
- "game-changing" / "game changer"
- "revolutionise" / "transform your business"
- "seamlessly"
- "leverage" (as a verb)
- "world-class"
- "best-in-class"
- Em dashes (—) and en dashes (–) — use commas or rewrite
- Exclamation marks in headings

## Good Headline Patterns
- "We build [specific thing] for [specific audience]"
- "Your first [timeframe] of [outcome]"
- "[Verb] [outcome] — not [what others do]"
- Questions: "Why Zutomate?" not "Why choose us?"

## Badge / Label Text (ALL CAPS)
- "WHY ZUTOMATE"
- "HOW WE WORK"
- "WHAT OUR CLIENTS SAY"
- "SERVICES"
- "FOR B2B BUSINESSES"
- "NOT JUST AUTOMATION"

## CTA Copy
- Primary: "Book a Call"
- Secondary: "Get Started", "See How It Works"
- Avoid: "Learn More", "Click Here", "Submit"

## Testimonial Trim Rules
- Max 2-3 sentences per card
- Keep specific results if mentioned ("grabbed thousands of leads", "5 years")
- Remove: "I would highly recommend" openers — keep the substance
- Remove: repetitive praise words — keep specific observations

## Services Naming
- GTM Systems — AI Outbound Engine, LinkedIn Growth, ABM Signal Engine
- AI Agent Systems — AI RAG Agent, Lead Qualification Agent
- Automated CRM Systems — CRM Lifecycle Engine, CRM Pipeline Engine

## Company Positioning Line
"We're not a Clay agency. We embed AI-powered growth systems directly into your operations."
